## Step 2 — Name the outcome family

Classify the outcome as exactly one of these:

### 1. Explicit bounded failure
Use when:
- durable state says failure
- success evidence was not emitted
- failure is explicit, not inferred from absence

### 2. Replay-safe bounded no-op
Use when:
- replay or repeat invocation reused already-landed truth
- no duplicate success evidence was written

### 3. Healed interrupted split-success
Use when:
- interruption split a bounded success family across surfaces
- replay healed the missing side without duplicating evidence

### 4. Acceptable bounded filtering
Use when:
- exclusion is intentional eligibility filtering
- not malformed truth silently disappearing

### 5. Under-classified ambiguity debt
Use when:
- deterministic behavior exists
- but malformed or incomplete truth disappears into absence without governed explanation

### 6. Prevented contradiction
Use when:
- the contradiction class is explicitly blocked in bounded tested form

### 7. Partial / under-centralized family
Use when:
- bounded proof exists
- but the family is still fragmented and not governable from one operator-facing surface
